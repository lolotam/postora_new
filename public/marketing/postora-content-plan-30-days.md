## Goal

Increase qualified website visits and signups by showing three audiences why Postora is useful:

- Creators and small businesses who need simple scheduling.
- Marketers and agencies who need multi-account workflows, analytics, and approvals.
- Developers and automation users who want API, MCP, and n8n posting.

- Free plan: $0, 3 connected accounts, 2 social profiles, 30 posts/month, 1 post/day, 20 media uploads/day, API access, image tools, standard support.

- Pricing page offer: Early Bird Pro for $5/month or $99.99/year for the first 100 users.
- Yearly billing message: save up to 17%.

- Multi-platform posting: Instagram, Facebook, Threads, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, X, LinkedIn, Bluesky, Reddit.

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram

Audience: creators, small business owners, solo marketers, visual-first brands.  
Content style: polished visuals, carousels, quick reels, before/after workflow posts.  
Primary CTAs: Start free, try Pro offer, connect your Instagram/Facebook/Threads accounts.  
Best formats: carousel explainers, 15-30 second reels, square product visuals, Story polls.

### Facebook Page

Audience: small business owners, local businesses, founders, agencies, decision makers.  
Content style: benefit-led posts, longer explanations, screenshots, testimonials, offer posts.  
Primary CTAs: Visit pricing, start free, upgrade to Pro, read docs for automation.  
Best formats: feed posts, link posts, short videos, pinned offer post.

### Threads

Audience: marketers, founders, SaaS builders, creators, automation-minded users.  
Content style: short insights, direct questions, mini threads, build-in-public product notes.  
Primary CTAs: Try the tool, comment for workflow, visit docs/MCP, start free.  
Best formats: text-first hooks, short tactical threads, quick comparison posts.

## Content Pillars

### 1. Pain Point To Solution

Use for creators and small businesses.

- Hook: "Still posting manually every day?"
- Message: Postora gives you a calendar, scheduling, and multi-platform publishing.
- CTA: "Start free at postora.cloud."

### 2. AI Productivity

Use for creators, marketers, and agencies.

- Hook: "Blank caption box? Start with AI."
- Message: Generate captions, hashtags, and images, then schedule from the same workspace.
- CTA: "Try Pro or the Early Bird offer."

### 3. Automation And API

Use for developers, technical marketers, and agencies.

- Hook: "Your content workflow can run from an API."
- Message: Postora supports API, MCP, n8n, media upload, post creation, scheduling, and webhooks.
- CTA: "Open `/api-keys`, `/docs/mcp-server`, or `/n8n`."

### 4. Trust And Compliance

Use for business owners and agencies.

- Hook: "You should never share social passwords with tools."
- Message: Postora uses secure OAuth and platform-specific settings.
- CTA: "Connect accounts securely."

### 5. Offer And Conversion

Use 2-3 times per week, not daily.

- Hook: "Early Bird Pro is open."
- Message: Pro is available for $5/month or $99.99/year for the first 100 users.
- CTA: "Upgrade from `/pricing` before spots are gone."
