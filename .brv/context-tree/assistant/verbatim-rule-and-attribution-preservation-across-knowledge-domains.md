# Verbatim Rule and Attribution Preservation Across Knowledge Domains

A strict standard for verbatim rule and attribution preservation is enforced in both assistant curation and project management retrospectives, ensuring that all operational rules, lists, and action items are complete, traceable, and auditable with explicit author and temporal metadata. This cross-domain alignment guarantees reliable process improvement and supports robust audit trails.

## Evidence

- **assistant**: Verbatim preservation, complete enumeration, and explicit attribution are mandatory for all curated entries; project management retros mirror these requirements (see rigorous-rule-and-attribution-preservation-across-processes.md, rule-preservation-and-attribution-a-cross-domain-standard.md).
- **project_management**: Retrospectives structure new rules and action items per the same standards of traceability and attribution established in assistant guidelines.
